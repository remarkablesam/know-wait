Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KnowEmpire and how it works.

What is KnowEmpire?

Know Empire is a Web3 peer-to-peer marketplace mini-app built on Farcaster that allows users to buy and sell both digital and physical goods, as well as offer freelance services. All transactions are secured through smart contract escrow.

Do I need a Farcaster account to use KnowEmpire?

Yes, Know Empire is built as a Farcaster mini-app, so you'll need a Farcaster account to access the platform. This integration provides identity verification and community trust through your existing Web3 social graph.

How does payment work?

When a buyer places an order, the payment (in USDC) is sent to a smart contract escrow, not directly to the seller. The seller only receives the funds after the buyer confirms the item or service was delivered.

What types of items can I sell on KnowEmpire?

You can sell both digital products (like artwork, templates, or files) and physical items (like t-shirts, prints, or handmade goods). You can also offer services such as design, writing, or marketing.

What happens if there’s a problem with the delivery?

We use the tracking ID, it will help resolve the issue using the shipment data. If no tracking was provided, the seller is fully responsible.

Can I be both a buyer and a seller?

Yes! You can buy and sell on KnowEmpire. It’s built to support both buyers and sellers all in one place.

Do I need a wallet to use Know Empire?

Yes, since Know Empire runs onchain, you’ll need a crypto wallet to sign in and handle payments.
