Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KnowEmpire and how it works.

What is KnowEmpire?

Know Empire is a Web3 peer-to-peer marketplace mini-app built on Farcaster that allows users to buy and sell both digital and physical goods, as well as offer freelance services. All transactions are secured through smart contract escrow.

Do I need a Farcaster account to use KnowEmpire?

Yes, Know Empire leverages Farcaster for user authentication and social interactions within the marketplace. You will need a Farcaster ID to get started.

How does payment work?

Payments are made using various cryptocurrencies supported on the platform. Funds are held in a secure escrow smart contract until the transaction terms are met and confirmed by both parties.

What types of items can I sell on KnowEmpire?

You can sell a wide range of items, including digital products (e.g., e-books, software licenses), physical goods, and offer various services (e.g., graphic design, writing, consulting).

What happens if there's a problem with the delivery?

Our escrow system includes a dispute resolution mechanism. If there's a problem, you can open a dispute, and our team will mediate to ensure a fair resolution based on the transaction terms.

Can I be both a buyer and a seller?

Absolutely! Know Empire is designed for users to seamlessly transition between buying and selling roles, allowing you to participate fully in the marketplace ecosystem.

Do I need a wallet to use Know Empire?

Yes, a compatible Web3 wallet is required to interact with the smart contracts, manage your funds, and participate in transactions on the Know Empire platform.
